Rock Climbing Equipment

Alex Hannold - Professional Climber
Equipment:
  • Shoes: These are very important as they determine the amount of grip you get while rock climbing.
  • Harness: A harness is what you tie a rope or belay device into in order to climb walls.
  • Chalk: This is a necessity for everyone because it keeps your hands dry and slip-free.
